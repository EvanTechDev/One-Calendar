Gain insights into your scheduling patterns with built-in analytics.

## Overview

The Analytics view provides visualizations and metrics for your calendar events. Access it from the sidebar or view switcher.

## Metrics Dashboard

- **Longest Streak**: Consecutive days with scheduled events
- **Busiest Weekday**: Most active day of the week
- **Avg Lead Days**: How far in advance you schedule
- **Peak Time Window**: Most active 2-hour window

## Charts

### Daily/Monthly Event Count

Bar chart showing event frequency over time. Toggle between daily and monthly views.

### Category Distribution (Donut)

See what percentage of events belong to each calendar category.

### Average Duration by Category

Compare average event duration across different categories.

### Weekday Stacked Duration

View total hours scheduled per weekday, broken down by category.

### Year Heatmap

GitHub-style contribution heatmap showing event density across the year.

## Date Range Presets

Filter analytics by:

- Week
- Month
- Quarter

## Use Cases

- Identify your most productive days
- Track work-life balance
- Measure scheduling consistency
- Optimize time allocation across categories
